Planet OM

Planet OM is the sonic vessel of Gidi Cohen, an Israeli keyboardist and producer.

It is where cinematic production meets global heritage. Electronic pulses intertwine with Middle Eastern scales and psychedelic textures to create harmonious landscapes.

Whether intended for meditation or movement, the sound of Planet OM is a bridge between the ancient and the synthetic.
