New album

AUTOT

Full album released · 01.01.2025

AUTOT is the debut album of Planet OM. The album cover showcases a work by the late Israeli painter David Rakia, taken from his iconic "Signs" series ("Autot" - אותות in Hebrew), which presents a mystical perspective on existence.

Planet OM combines a wide range of genres with elements from electronic music and Middle Eastern psychedelic influences, in collaboration with other Israeli musicians.

EP release

On the album cover: the work "Struggle" by the late painter David Rakia. The music is inspired by the painting and by the periodic struggle in which we find ourselves.

The project

About Planet OM

Led by Israeli keyboardist and producer Gidi Cohen, Planet OM offers a cinematic journey through a world where electronic music intertwines with global influences.

This fusion creates grand, harmonious landscapes intended to transport listeners to otherworldly realms. Each track is a vibrant exploration of sound that moves between meditative spaces and rhythm focused sections.

Om, the sound of creation, embraces a wide variety of styles, whether you want to unwind, dance or explore new musical horizons.

Planet OM - studio crafted electronic world music from Israel.
